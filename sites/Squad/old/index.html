Squad connects the world through sports

Our team at Squad is passionate about sports, and we've created a social sports network with immersive media content that millions of monthly users enjoy to experience sports through the perspective of athletes, execs, and fans.

Bootstrap Professionals

Sports is being shared on Snapchat, Vine, Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram, but there's no centralized community for athletes, execs, and fans. Squad's goal is the world’s largest sports network of 100M+ global members, which is attainable by leveraging hundreds of relationships with professional sports teams and live event partners through TeamWork. These relationships include sports and live event partners in professional football, basketball, baseball, hockey, soccer, mixed martial arts, stock car racing, golf, tennis, college sports, and many more.
