We are ending the silence.

Learn How

Where you find passionate people, anything is possible.
The creative community we found in the midst of our Kickstarter campaigns has enabled us to provide high quality sound effects to the internet, completely free of charge. One free library at a time, we are ending the silence.

Still North Media LLC is the product of that incredible opportunity, and while we continue to produce open source audio content, we are also putting our creative passion and our libraries to work in film production.
